Overview

What happened, in three sentences

A well-established landscaping company in a competitive suburban market had reached a growth ceiling after relying almost entirely on referrals for years. Over 10 months we rebuilt their website as a lead generation engine, executed a local SEO program targeting 50+ high-intent keywords, and optimized their Google Business Profile with a complete service menu and weekly content cadence.

The result was a 4.8X increase in qualified leads — from 10 to 48 per month — while organic traffic grew from 420 to 5,840 monthly visits. Revenue influenced by digital channels rose to $18,500 per month, up from $4,200.

FAQ

Common questions

How long did this project take?

The full engagement ran 10 months, from the initial technical audit through post-launch conversion optimization.

How long before rankings improved?

First movement in local pack rankings appeared around month 3, with the largest gains between months 4 and 7 after content production and citation cleanup were complete.

Which service had the biggest impact?

The website rebuild and conversion optimization produced the fastest revenue impact. Local SEO and content marketing compounded over the full engagement and drove the majority of organic traffic by month 8.

How many keywords did you target?

We targeted 50+ high-intent keywords across landscaping, hardscaping, irrigation, lawn care, and seasonal services. The business ranked in the local pack for 42 of those by month 10.

What tools were used?

See the Tools & Platforms section above for the full stack — built on Next.js with Cloudflare infrastructure, GA4 for analytics, and Ahrefs for keyword tracking.

How were results measured?

Lead and revenue data came directly from the client's CRM tied to form submissions and phone calls. Traffic and ranking data came from Google Analytics 4, Search Console, and Ahrefs position tracking.
